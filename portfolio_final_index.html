About Me

Why I Build Robots

From Curiosity to Engineering

My passion for robotics began when I realised that the gap between "what robots can do" and "what we need them to do" is simply engineering problems waiting to be solved.

I don't just write code — I build complete systems. From a robotic arm sorting samples with millimetre precision to a drone responding to hand gestures in real time, I turn complex challenges into working solutions.

What drives me? The moment hardware, software, and intelligence converge perfectly.
